Jos De Roo

Researcher at Agfa HealthCare and developer of Euler open source.

Bio

Jos De Roo graduated in 1979 as Master of Science (ir.) at KU Leuven
and did a doctoral study on image computing from 1979 till 1982.

He was co-founder of ICOS Vision Systems in 1982 and worked on
computer vision applications until 1985.

Since 1985 he works at Agfa HealtCare as researcher in the successive
areas of digital medical hardcopy, medical gateways, advanced clinical
applications and clinical analytics.

He is also developer of Euler open source.
